About Me

Being a nerd at heart, I always love to challenge myself. At the age of 7, I started playing video games after watching my Father play Diablo 2 from Blizzard Entertainment. This became my first game I played and made a running start with my love for video games. My favorite genres being RPGs and Strategy, but I always love branching out. I play mainly on the PC, but I also grew up with Nintendo systems. My first game on a Nintendo system was Legend of Zelda: Spirit Tracks, in which it sparked my love for the rest of the series.

I am interested in Dungeons and Dragons (though really Pathfinder is my go-to), Pok√©mon card collecting, art, story writing, and when I am alone or just in a joyous mood: singing! I am definitely a nerd to the core (and a kid at heart), but I will lock in and become serious when the time is needed!

When it comes to inspiration, fantasy and unique creatures have always inspired me with design. I love to take the norm and put a twist on it, or even completely change it up. I do heavy research into my work for inspiration, whether it be for landscapes, characters, or story settings. It helps my thought process and allows my ideas to come to life.
